What is PNS2?

A name that goes
everywhere you do.

Your wallet address looks like 5GrwvaEF…. Nobody remembers that. PNS2 lets you claim a name — like alice.substrate.icu — that points to your wallet and anything else you want to attach to it.

And unlike other naming services, PNS2 names are real DNS. Someone on a regular browser can visit alice.substrate.icu and land on your page. No crypto knowledge required. No extension to install. It just resolves.

substrate.icu is the root. Every name under it is a PNS2 name — living on-chain, owned by you, resolvable by anyone.
